Hummus Recipe

Hummus Recipe! YAY!

Description

It taste good and it's like a dip. It is one of the tastiest dips! It has lots of protien, I think. I use it with tortillas and it taste great!

Ingredients

  • 1(15oz) can chickpeas, drained
  • 3 tablespoons tahini
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin(Optional)
  • 1 pinch paprika
  • 1 sprig fress parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Blend the hell out of some chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, water, garlic, and cumin for a minute. Put it in a bowl sprinkle some paprika and fresh parsley on that stuff!
  2. DIG IN BROSKI!
